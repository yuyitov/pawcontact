Data we collect

Directly from you, through Stripe and the intake form hosted on Tally:

  • Identification and contact: name or brand name, email, phone or WhatsApp.
  • About the business: your business name, logo, photos, services with prices, packages or promos, hours, location and contact links (WhatsApp, Instagram, Maps, booking)

Primary: generate and publish your page, deliver it by email, handle corrections and support, and meet legal and tax obligations. Secondary (optional): send you notices about service improvements. You can opt out by writing to hello@pawcontact.com.

The business information and photos you send are published on your page, which is public by the nature of the service you buy.

We use providers that process data on our behalf and may process it in the United States or other countries: Stripe (payments), Tally (intake form and uploads), Cloudflare (infrastructure and publishing), GitHub (generation and hosting of the code and pages), SendGrid (email) and OpenAI (automatic translation of your page's text into the site's other language). We do not sell your data and do not transfer it to third parties for their own purposes.

Your rights (ARCO)

You may exercise access, rectification, cancellation or objection, and revoke your consent, by writing to hello@pawcontact.com with your name and the URL of your page. We respond within the timeframes set by the LFPDPPP. You can also use your correction link to update your page.

Retention

We keep your data while your page is published and for as long as legal and tax obligations require. If you request cancellation, we take the page down and delete the data not subject to legal retention.

Minors

The service is intended for businesses and adults; we do not knowingly collect data from minors.
