FAQ

Straight answers

No inflated promises — here's what PawContact does and doesn't do.

Does it include online booking?

No. Booking and contact happen through WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Maps, or your external booking link if you already use one. There's no built-in booking system — your page sends pet parents straight to you.

Can clients pay a deposit on the page?

Not today. Your page shows your prices clearly so there are no surprises, but you collect payment the way you do now.

Is this a grooming management system, like Moego or Gingr?

No — and that's the point. Those are full systems for staff schedules, medical records, and boarding operations. PawContact is one page that shows your work, your services, and your prices, so people stop asking “how much?” in your DMs. If you need a full system, keep your full system.

I already use Moego / Gingr / PetDesk — why would I buy this?

Because it does a different job. Your booking software manages appointments; this is the page you send people before they book. Plenty of shops use both.

Is it just a QR code?

No. The QR is one way to share it. What you get is a full service page — services, prices, promos, hours, location, contact buttons — plus a shareable link and the QR.

Do I need an app?

No. Neither you nor your clients download anything. It works in any browser, on any phone.

Can I use it in my Instagram bio?

Yes. That's where most of our pages live.

Can I change my prices later?

Yes. One free modification is included. After that, extra changes are $6 each.

Can I pick my own colors?

You choose from 4 curated styles designed for pet businesses. No free-form color picking — that's how we guarantee your page always looks good.

Do you serve Spanish-speaking clients?

Every page is bilingual automatically. Your English- and Spanish-speaking pet parents each see the page in their language, from the same link.

What if I don't love my page?

First we fix it for free — if anything is off on our end, we correct it at no cost. If you still don't love it, you get your money back.

When can I buy it?

Right now. Click “Get my PawContact,” pay, and fill out a short form — we build and publish your page shortly after.
